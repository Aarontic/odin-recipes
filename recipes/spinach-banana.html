Spinach and Banana Smoothie

Description

This is another healthy option with some added greens! The banana is for sweetness and combined with the spinach makes for a high energy packed smoothie to take on the day!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain soy milk
  • 3/4 cup packed fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 large banana, sliced

Directions

  1. Blend soy milk and spinach together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Add banana and pulse until thoroughly blended.
